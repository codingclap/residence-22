The Concept

An Unrivaled Standard of Urban Living

Residence 22 is masterfully designed for those who believe sharing a floor is for hotels, not homes. By seamlessly blending five-star sophistication with absolute residential privacy, we have created an urban oasis that stands absolute by the creek. With only 19 penthouse suites—each claiming its own dedicated floor—this address serves as a lavish island of tranquility for the few who value space as the ultimate luxury.
